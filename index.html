No Backlog!

Value > Efficiency

The 'product backlog' as a tool has outlived its usefulness. It's time to focus on what's important and valuable versus efficiency and 'keeping the engineers busy'

There is nothing quite so useless as doing with great efficiency something that should not be done at all.

Peter Drucker
Management Guru

First Principles

Value is determined by the customer
All too often a stakeholder or another team will request you to add a feature to your backlog because they need it. Or a HIPPO will fancy a feature they saw on a competitive product and ask you to 'add it to your backlog'. We need to anchor to problems we're solving for the customer.
We need understand the customers context and problems
Are we talking with our customers on a regular candence? Do we understand what they are trying to accomplish in their life? Why are they looking for a solution? Why now?
Customers don't care about your shiny technology or latest feature
It's about them, not us. Not everyone in the organization understands this, but our teams need to.
